Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sioux falls known for?

Sioux Falls is known for many things, some of them being: beautiful natural scenery, exotic flowers, and lots of restaurants.

Is there anything worth seeing in South Dakota?

Of Course, there are countless sites to see and topping the list is Falls Park.

Is South Dakota's cost of living above or below the average in the US?

The cost of living in South Dakota is on the lower side of the national average but just barely.

Where did Sioux Falls get its name?

Sioux Falls got its name from the Sioux tribe of Native Americans and the big Sioux River.
